chess game
This game has two modes 
1)player vs player
2)player vs computer
you can play by clicking on any piece and
again on the path which gets highlighted to blue
square,the game ends when king is either checkmate
or stalemate.checkmate can be identified when 
square of king in check turns red and the square
of pieces attacking turns pink.Stalemate can be
identified when king's square does not turns red
but attacking pieces square turns pink.
